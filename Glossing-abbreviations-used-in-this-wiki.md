This is a list of glossing abbreviations used in the examples in this wiki. We followed the conventions of the [Leipzig Glossing Rules](https://www.eva.mpg.de/lingua/pdf/Glossing-Rules.pdf). When these rules did not have a suggestion for a particular gloss, we referred to [this list](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_glossing_abbreviations) of glossing abbreviations, assembled from various reference sources. When there was no conventional gloss, we followed the conventions used in the sources cited in the individual wiki articles. In some cases, we also explain the abbreviation in the wiki articles.
